Beijing Day 6 - Drupal Tour 2016

  • Share
  • Tweet
  • +1

Taking a break from sightseen activities, yesterday I visited the offices of Think Drupal.

I have the chance to discuss some technical details about development in Drupal 8 with one of their developers, and also, they explain to me some of the more relevant projects.

They build the largest traffic Drupal website in Asia http://e.baidu.com/ with 1 million unique visitors per day. This site the Chinese version of Google AdWords.

In Drupal 8 segment, they show an intranet they built Chinese office of patents and a new website they are building for Human resources development center OF satcm.



Continuing with software aspect, we visited a compound named Zhongguancun Software Park which is like Silicon Valley in Beijing for biggest Internet companies in China in combination with some IT global companies like Oracle, IBM, etc.
